Label 3
Phase: Porous NMC811 cathode
Formula / mixture used: Effective attenuating phase: LiNi0.8Mn0.1Co0.1O2; binder, carbon and electrolyte ignored
Density used: 2.61 g cm^-3
Notes: Represents a commercial porous NMC811 cathode coating. Assumes 90 wt% NMC811 in the dry solid coating and 30% porosity. Binder, carbon and electrolyte are ignored for attenuation, so the density is the effective NMC811 mass per unit coating volume, not the full coating bulk density and not the dense crystal density.

Notes: This approximates a partial-volume Cu voxel whose greyscale/attenuation is similar to cathode material. Equivalent to roughly 20% Cu and 80% void/background/other by volume.
